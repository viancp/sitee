About Me

A student of the Business Statistics Department and also the CEO and owner of OC Jacket. Through OC Jacket, I am committed to delivering products that are not only of high quality but also comfortable to use. As the leader of this business, I am directly involved in all operational aspects—from product planning, design, production management, marketing, to customer service—with the goal of building a professional, sustainable, and consumer-oriented business..

On the other hand, I am also active in various campus committees as a tangible form of my contribution to the academic and social environment at the university. This involvement not only serves as a platform for me to play a role in the success of various campus activities but also as a means of personal development outside of academics and business. Through this experience, I have learned to build effective communication, broaden my knowledge across disciplines, strengthen friendships and professional networks, and sharpen my managerial, leadership, and problem-solving skills in dynamic and collaborative situations.
